Frequently asked questions

Questions about choosing us.

Is Language Access Hub a staffing agency or a full-service company? +

We are a full-service language access company — not a staffing agency. We manage the entire workflow: project intake, linguist assignment, quality review, formatting, delivery, and compliance documentation. You interact with a project manager and receive a finished product, not a roster of contractors to manage yourself.

What's the difference between GSA and NASPO contracts? +

GSA (General Services Administration) Schedule contracts are for federal agencies. Any federal department or agency can issue a task order against our GSA contract without a separate competitive bid. NASPO ValuePoint is the equivalent cooperative contract for state and local government agencies — states issue a Participating Addendum, and local agencies within that state can then use the contract. Both vehicles eliminate the need for a new competitive procurement.

Do you work with private sector clients too? +

Yes. While much of our work is with government agencies, school districts, and healthcare systems, we also serve law firms, hospitals, corporate clients, and nonprofits. Private sector clients order directly online or request a custom quote. The same quality standards apply regardless of sector.

Are you HIPAA compliant? +

Yes. We execute Business Associate Agreements (BAAs) as a standard part of healthcare contracting — not as a special request. Our HIPAA compliance program includes workforce training, minimum necessary access controls, encrypted data transmission (TLS 1.3), AES-256 storage encryption, and documented breach notification procedures. Healthcare clients can request a BAA at any time before or during the contracting process.

How do I start? +

Government agencies can start by issuing a task order against our existing GSA or NASPO contract — no new procurement required. Private sector and direct clients can submit a quote request using the form on our homepage, or order translation directly online. We respond to all inquiries within 2 business hours.
