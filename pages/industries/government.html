Title VI & EO 13166

Requires meaningful language access for LEP individuals in all federally assisted programs. Includes translation of vital documents and provision of qualified interpreter services. Our Language Access Plans (LAPs) document your compliance.

Section 508 & ADA Title II

Federal agencies: Section 508 applies to all ICT. State & local governments: ADA Title II WCAG 2.1 AA deadline is April 2026. We provide full audits, PDF remediation, VPAT creation, and ongoing monitoring.
