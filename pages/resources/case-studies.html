Case Study 01 Government · Title VI

State Health Department — Title VI OCR Investigation

Mid-size state health department (unnamed, Southeast US)

The Challenge

Serving a county with 40% Spanish-speaking and 12% Vietnamese-speaking LEP population with no formal Language Access Plan. After a patient complaint about lack of interpreter access in a public health clinic, an OCR (Office for Civil Rights) investigation was opened under Title VI.

What We Delivered

  • ✓Full Language Access Plan development consultation
  • ✓Translation of 14 vital documents into Spanish and Vietnamese
  • ✓Ongoing OPI contract for clinic staff
  • ✓Documentation of interpreter dispatch protocol

Case Study 03 Healthcare · Section 504/1557

Regional Hospital System — Section 504/1557 Compliance

Regional hospital system, 3 facilities (unnamed, Mid-Atlantic)

The Challenge

Hospital needed to achieve Section 504 of the Rehabilitation Act and Section 1557 of the ACA compliance before the May 2026 HHS deadline. They had interpretation coverage but no BAA in place for written translation, and no systematic approach to translated patient documents.

What We Delivered

  • ✓HIPAA BAA executed
  • ✓Audit of all patient-facing documents
  • ✓Translation of 28 priority documents (consent forms, discharge instructions, medication guides) into Spanish, Arabic, and Haitian Creole
  • ✓24/7 OPI for emergency department
  • ✓WCAG 2.1 audit for patient portal
