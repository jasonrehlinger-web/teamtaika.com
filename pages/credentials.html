Common questions

Procurement FAQ

Can we sole-source this contract as a VOSB set-aside?

Yes, for qualifying acquisitions under applicable thresholds. Taika Translations LLC is SBA VOSB certified. Contact your contracting officer to confirm your agency's set-aside threshold and procedures. We can provide all required documentation.

How do I verify your GSA contract status?

You can verify our GSA Multiple Award Schedule status at GSA Advantage (gsaadvantage.gov) or through the GSA eLibrary. You can also request a copy of our GSA contract documentation directly at sales@taikatranslations.com.

Do you have a HIPAA Business Associate Agreement available?

Yes. We execute HIPAA BAAs as standard practice for all healthcare clients. The BAA is available upon request before any healthcare-related work begins. Email sales@taikatranslations.com to initiate.

Are your translations accepted by USCIS and federal courts?

Yes. Our certified translations include a signed translator competency declaration meeting USCIS requirements (8 CFR 103.2(b)(3)). We have a 100% acceptance rate with USCIS, federal and state courts, academic institutions, and federal agencies.

What is your SAM.gov registration status?

Taika Translations LLC maintains an active SAM.gov registration as required for federal contract eligibility. Registration can be verified at sam.gov using our legal company name.

Do you handle multi-year task orders?

Yes. We support base year plus option year structures, IDIQ arrangements, and blanket purchase agreements (BPAs) under our GSA and NASPO contracts.

Need a documentation package?

We can prepare a complete credential packet — SAM registration, VOSB certificate, NASPO/GSA contract info, HIPAA BAA, and sample SOW — for your contracting office.
